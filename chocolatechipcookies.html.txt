      <h2>Chocolate Chip Cookies</h2>
      
      <div class="container">
        <div><img src="ccc.jpg" alt="chocolate chip cookies" title="Chocolate chip cookie recipe" width="300" height="300"></div>
        <div><h4>Ingredients (Makes 8–10 large cookies)</h4>
          <ul>
            <li>½ tsp salt</li>
            <li>1 tsp baking soda</li>
            <li>1 cup (200g) brown sugar</li>
            <li>2 ½ cups (315g) all-purpose flour</li>
            <li>1 cup (227g) unsalted butter, softened</li>
            <li>½ cup (100g) granulated sugar</li>
            <li>chocolate chips</li>
            <li>2 cups (340g) semisweet chocolate chips (or chunks)</li>
            <li>2 tsp vanilla extract</li>
            
          <h4>Optional:</h4>
          
           <li>½ cup (60g) chopped nuts (walnuts or pecans)</li>
           <li>Sprinkle of flaky sea salt on top before baking for added flavor</li>
           </ul></div>
      </div>
      
      
      <!--Recipe-->
      <h3> Instructions</h3>
      
      <!--Step 1-->
      <h5>1. Prepare the dough</h5>
      <p>-Beet 1 cup (227g) softened unsalted butter, 1 cup (200g) brown sugar, and ½ cup (100g) granulated sugar until light and fluffy.<br>
      -Add 2 tsp vanilla extract.<br>
      -Add 2 eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.<br></p>
      
      <!--Step 2-->
      <h5>2. Combine dry ingredients</h5>
      <p>-Whisk together 2½ cups (315g) all-purpose flour, 1 tsp baking soda, and ½ tsp salt in a separate bowl.<br>
      -Gradually add the dry mixture to the wet ingredients, mixing until just combined.<br></p>
      
      <!--Step 3-->
      <h5>3. Add mix-ins</h5>
      <p>-Fold in 2 cups (340g) semisweet chocolate chips (or chunks).<br>
      -Optionally, add ½ cup (60g) chopped nuts (walnuts or pecans).<br>
      -Chill the dough for 30 minutes (optional).<br></p>
      
      <!--Step 4-->
      <h5>4. Bake the cookies</h5>
      <p>-Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F).<br>
      -Scoop large dough balls (~¼ cup) onto a parchment-lined baking sheet.<br>
      -Optionally sprinkle with flaky sea salt.<br>
      -Bake for 12–15 minutes until edges are golden but centers still soft.<br></p>
      
      <!--Step 5-->
      <h5>5. Cool and enjoy</h5>
      <p>-Let cookies cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes.<br>
      -Transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.<br>
      -Serve warm or store in an airtight container for up to 5 days.<br></p>
      
      <h3>Bon appetit!</h3>
