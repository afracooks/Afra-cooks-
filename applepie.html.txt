      <h2>Apple pie </h2>
      <div  class="container">
        <div id="afbeelding"> <img src="applepie2.jpg" alt="apple pie" title="Apple pie" width="300" height="300"></div>
        <div><h4>Ingredients</h4>
          <ul> 
            <li>1½ cups graham cracker crumbs</li>
            <li>6 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted</li>
            <li>2 tablespoons granulated sugar</li>
            <li>pie crust</li>
            <li>5–6 medium apples (Granny Smith or Honeycrisp), peeled, cored, and sliced</li>
            <li>½ cup granulated sugar</li>
            <li>1 teaspoon ground cinnamon</li>
            <li>¼ teaspoon ground nutmeg</li>
            <li>1 tablespoon lemon juice</li>
            <li>1 tablespoon all-purpose flour</li>
            <li>2 tablespoons unsalted butter, cut into small cubes</li>
            <li>¾ cup all-purpose flour</li>
            <li>½ cup brown sugar, packed</li>
            <li>1 teaspoon ground cinnamon</li>
            <li>Pinch of salt</li>
            <li> 6 tablespoons unsalted butter, cold and cubed</li>
          </ul></div>
        </div>   
      
         <!--Recipe-->
      <h4>Instructions</h4>
      <!--Step 1-->
      <h5>1. Prepare the crust</h5>
      
      <p>-Mix 1½ cups of graham cracker crumbs, 6 tbsp melted butter, and 2 tbsp sugar.<br>
      
      -Press the mixture into the bottom and sides of a 9-inch pie dish.<br>
      
      -Bake at 175°C (350°F) for 8–10 minutes. Let it cool. </p>
      
       <!--Step 2-->
       
      <h5>2. Make the apple filling</h5>
      
      <p>-Peel, core, and slice 5–6 medium apples (like Granny Smith or Honeycrisp).<br>
      
      -In a large bowl, toss apples with ½ cup sugar, 1 tsp cinnamon, ¼ tsp nutmeg, 1 tbsp lemon juice, and 1 tbsp flour.<br>
      
      -Let it sit for 10 minutes to draw out juices.</p>
      
       <!--Step 3-->
       
      <h5>3. Assamble the pie </h5>
      
      <p>-Spoon the apple mixture into the cooled crust, spreading evenly.<br>
      
      -Dot the top with small cubes of 2 tbsp cold butter.</p>
      
       <!--Step 4-->
       
      <h5>4. Make the crumb topping</h5>
      
      <p>-In a bowl, mix ¾ cup flour, ½ cup brown sugar, 1 tsp cinnamon, and a pinch of salt.<br>
      
      -Add 6 tbsp cold butter, cut into cubes, and rub with your fingers until crumbly.<br>
      
      -Sprinkle the topping over the apples.<br>
      
      </p>
      
       <!--Step 5-->
       
      <h5>5. Bake the pie</h5>
      
      <p>-Bake at 175°C (350°F) for 45–55 minutes, until the topping is golden and the apples are tender.<br>
      
      -If the top browns too quickly, cover loosely with foil during the last 15 minutes.</p>
      <!--Step 7 -->
      <h5>6. Cool and serve</h5>
       
      -Let the pie cool at room temperature for at least 2 hours.<br>
      
      -Serve warm or chilled, optionally with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream.<br>
      
      <h3>Bon appétit!</h3>
