        <h2>Cheesecake </h2>  
        <div class="container"> 
          <div><img src="cheesecake2.png" alt="cheesecake" title="Cheesecake" width="300" height="300"></div>
          <div>
            <h3>Ingredients</h3>
            <ul>
              <li> 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs</li>
              <li>¼ cup melted butter</li>
              <li>2 tbsp sugar</li>
              <li>brown sugar</li>
              <li>¾ cup sugar</li>
              <li>1 tsp Vanilla extract</li>
              <li>2 cups cream cheese (room temperature)</li>
              <li>2 Eggs</li>
              <li>½ cup sour cream</li>
              <li>2 tbsp Milk</li>
              <li>2 tbsp Flour</li>
              <li>Mixed red fruits (fresh or frozen)</li>
            </ul>
          </div>
       </div>  
        
          <!--Recipe-->
        <h3>Instructions</h3>
        
        <!--Step 1-->
        <h4>1. Prepare the crust</h4>
        <p>-Mix graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and sugar.<br>
        -Press the mixture into the bottom of a springform pan.<br>
        -Bake at 175°C (350°F) for 10 minutes. Let it cool. </p>
        
        <!--Step 2-->
        <h4>2. Make the filling</h4>
        <p>-In a bowl, beat cream cheese and sugar until smooth.<br>
        -Add eggs one at a time, mixing well.<br>
        -Add vanilla, sour cream, flour, and milk. Mix until creamy.</p>
        
         <!--Step 3-->
        <h4>3. Bake the cheesecake </h4>
        
        <p>-Pour the filling over the crust.<br>
        -Bake at 160°C (320°F) for about 50–60 minutes, until the center is just set.<br>
        -Turn off the oven, slightly open the door, and let the cheesecake cool inside for 1 hour.</p>
        
         <!--Step 4-->
        <h4>4. Chill</h4>
        <p>-Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight.</p>
        
         <!--Step 5-->
        <h4>5. Top with the red fruits</h4>
        <p>-Before serving, add red fruits on top.</p>
        <h3>Bon appétit!</h3>
